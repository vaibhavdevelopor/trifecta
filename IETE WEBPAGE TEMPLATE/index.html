Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) Movies

Iron Man (2008)

Billionaire Tony Stark builds a suit of armor to escape captivity and becomes Iron Man, a hero for a new age.

The Incredible Hulk (2008)

Scientist Bruce Banner must harness his inner rage to control the Hulk, a powerful green alter-ego, while on the run from the military.

Iron Man 2 (2010)

Tony Stark faces new challenges as his technology attracts dangerous enemies, including the vengeful Ivan Vanko and corporate rival Justin Hammer.

Thor (2011)

The Norse god Thor is banished to Earth, where he learns humility and faces the threat of his brother Loki's ambitions.

Captain America: The First Avenger (2011)

Steve Rogers, a frail man transformed into a super-soldier, fights the Nazis as Captain America during World War II.

The Avengers (2012)

Earth's mightiest heroes, including Iron Man, Thor, and Captain America, join forces to stop Loki and an alien invasion.

Iron Man 3 (2013)

Tony Stark faces his biggest test yet as he battles the mysterious Mandarin and deals with PTSD from the events in New York.

Thor: The Dark World (2013)

Thor must save the universe from the dark forces of the Dark Elves, led by the vengeful Malekith.

Captain America: The Winter Soldier (2014)

Captain America uncovers a conspiracy within S.H.I.E.L.D. and confronts the mysterious Winter Soldier, a ghost from his past.

Guardians of the Galaxy (2014)

A group of misfit criminals must work together to stop a fanatical villain from using an all-powerful orb to destroy the galaxy.

Avengers: Age of Ultron (2015)

The Avengers face a powerful new foe, Ultron, a rogue AI created by Tony Stark, threatening to wipe out humanity.

Ant-Man (2015)

Thief Scott Lang becomes the tiny superhero Ant-Man, using a shrinking suit to pull off a heist and protect the technology from falling into the wrong hands.

Captain America: Civil War (2016)

The Avengers are split into opposing factions when political oversight is imposed, leading to a clash between Captain America and Iron Man.

Doctor Strange (2016)

After a car accident, neurosurgeon Stephen Strange seeks healing and discovers the mystical arts, becoming Earth's protector against dark forces.

Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2 (2017)

The Guardians continue their cosmic adventures, battling new enemies while unraveling secrets about Peter Quill's father, Ego the Living Planet.

Spider-Man: Homecoming (2017)

Teenager Peter Parker juggles high school life and superhero duties while battling the villainous Vulture and trying to prove himself to Tony Stark.

Thor: Ragnarok (2017)

Thor must escape from Sakaar and prevent the prophesied apocalypse, Ragnarok, from destroying Asgard while facing his sister Hela and teaming up with Hulk.

Black Panther (2018)

After his father's death, T'Challa returns to Wakanda to become king and face a new threat from a rival faction challenging his right to the throne.

Avengers: Infinity War (2018)

The Avengers and their allies must assemble to stop the tyrannical Thanos from collecting all the Infinity Stones and wiping out half of all life in the universe.

Ant-Man and The Wasp (2018)

Scott Lang teams up with Hope van Dyne as Ant-Man and The Wasp to uncover secrets from their past while dealing with a new enemy.

Captain Marvel (2019)

Carol Danvers becomes Captain Marvel, a powerful hero caught in the middle of a galactic conflict, as she discovers her true origins and destiny.

Avengers: Endgame (2019)

The surviving Avengers work to reverse the devastation caused by Thanos and restore balance to the universe in a final showdown.

Spider-Man: Far From Home (2019)

Peter Parker goes on a school trip to Europe but must take on a new threat, Mysterio, while grappling with the legacy of Tony Stark.
